Account Help - Register

How to register?

You click on the Register Link on top right of the page, simply fill in your name, email, and mobile number. Then create password, then press register button. Once you have registered, an email with an activation link will be sent to the email address you provided. Clicking on the activation link opens the account activation page.

Account Help - Login

How to login?

You click on the Login button on top right of the page, enter your email and password, then click the login button. You will be viewing your member's page.

Forgot password?

If you have forgotten your password, don't worry. You may reset it by clicking "Forgot my password". You are then required to enter your registered email address and click "Reset Password". An email notification will be sent to you. In the email you receive confirm your password reset by clicking on the reset password "link". It will direct you to enter your new password. If all steps are followed and completed, a successful password reset confirmation will be displayed. You will then be able to login with your new password.

Account Help - Account setting

How to manage changing my details?

When you change your details such as family name and phone number, login and go to my page. Click change detail button at the bottom of the page. Enter the new information and save the change.

Booking - Booking method

How to book?

Simply click on the "Booking" button at the top of the website. Choose the service you would like and a hair stylist. Select a time slot then press the booking button. Also an email notification will be sent to you including your appointment details.

Booking - Change booking details

How to Change booking details?

Login first then go to My Page, locate your current booking, and click the “Change booking” button. Then you can change the date and time or stylist. After you have made your changes press the “Update” button. If you do not have a login, call the salon and they will make the changes for you.

Can I change my booking at any time?

You can change your booking up to 24 hours in advance of your appointment both online or by calling the salon. If it is less than 24 hours this cannot be done online, please call the salon.

Booking - Cancel your booking

How to manage cancelling my booking?

You can cancel up to 24 hours in advance of your appointment both online or by calling the salon. If it is less than 24 hours this cannot be done online, please call the salon.
