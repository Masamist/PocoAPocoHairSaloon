Booking

Complimentry car parking

We ensure our customers have a superior experience overall so we also include complimentry car parking. Instructions for our customers to find utilise our carpark hassle. There are simple 3 steps.

1

Park your car in the most convenient car bay in Wilson Car Parking, located in our shops complex “newmarket rise”.

You also have more space behind the shop complex. Park in the “public parking”. You must not park in reserved parking spaces.

2

Come into Poco a poco and recieve your parking ticket.

If you are not sure you are parked in the correct place we will assist you to confirm the space is safe place to park.

3

Display a valid parking ticket onto your car window.

We can provide a parking ticket up to 30 minutes prior to your appointment. So you can enjoy shopping around or have a cup coffee at our lovely neighbour cafes “Cafe Monet” or “Cafe Brioche”.

We validate customers’ parking, We cannot cover any fine or issue.

Parking your car is your responsibility. Please make sure to come in the salon, recieve a parking ticket and display it after you park your car.
